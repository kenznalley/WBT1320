Spring Festivals

Egg Festival

The Egg festival takes place every Spring 13 in the town square as an Easter of sorts. You are given a time constraint to find eggs, and after time is up it is revealed who found the most. Pierre has a mini shop set up nearby during this time!

Flower Dance

The Flower dance is every Spring 24, and it takes place in the Cindersap Forest. You can invite any of the marriageable candidates to dance, but they will not agree unless you have 4 or more hearts. There is also a shop set up at the entrance, with some cutesy flower barrels and other decor.

Summer Festivals

Luau

The Luau happens every Summer on the 11th. It takes place on the beach, where the Governor comes into town to taste a pot of soup the others put together. You have the opportunity to put something in the pot to spice it up a little.

Dance of the Moonlight Jellies

The Dance of the Moonlight Jellies occurs on the last day of every summer. During the festival, villagers gather at the docks to watch migrating jellyfish who are attracted to the light of a torch that Mayor Lewis lights.

Fall Festivals

Stardew Valley Fair

The fair takes place on the 16th of every Fall, it contains games, a little shop, free survival burgers from Gus, and grange boxes for a competition between vendors. You bring items to display and go up against Pierre, Marnie, and Willy, and have the chance to win points to use at the little shop with more specialty items.

Spirit's Eve

Spirit's Eve is the games take on Halloween, however it includes a maze to get to a golden pumpkin. Pierre has a shop where he sells themed decor, including a rarecrow!

Winter Festivals

Festival of Ice

The Festival of Ice occurs in the Cindersap Forest, where there are igloos, snow people, and ice sculptures decorating the area. Theres also an ice fishing contest, where you can win neat prizes for obtaining the most fish.

Night Market

The Night Market is a 3 day market at the beach that features several shops, a mermaid show, and deep sea fishing.

Feast of the Winter Star

The Feast of the Winter Star acts as a stand in for Christmas, where you give a gift to a previously assigned NPC, and receive one from another NPC. Unfortunately these aren't always the best gifts... but they're gifts! The Feast also has a small shop with themed decor.
