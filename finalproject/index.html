Stardew Valley is a pixelated cozy farm game with all sorts of twists, turns and surprises. Your grandfather passes away and you inherit his farm in Pelican Town. You get tired of your corporate job and decide to move to the country to get away. Once in town, Lewis, the town mayor, and Robin, the woodsworker, meet you at the run down cabin amongst the overgrown field. Thus, your story begins.

With a little bit of time, dedication, and a lot of money, you might be able to work yourself up to restoring the Valley to its potential! Plant and harvest seasonal crops, raise farm animals, mine for minerals, fight monsters, make friends, court and marry, and much much more!!
