Marriageable Candidates

Abigail

Abigail is a spunky girl who lives at Pierre's General Store as she is "Pierre" and Caroline's daughter. She enjoys adventuring out, playing video games, and hanging out with her friends Sam and Sebastian. Her Birthday is Fall 13, and she visits Harvey's Clinic Spring 4. Her loved items are Amethyst, Pumpkins, Banana Pudding, Blackberry Cobbler, Chocolate Cake, Pufferfish, and Spicy Eel.

Haley

Haley is a blonde, superficial young woman who lives with her sister Emily in Pelican Town and enjoys photography. If you get to know her, she's gentle and kindhearted, and actually quite kind. Her birthday is Spring 14, and she visits Harvey's Clinic Winter 9. Her loved items are sunflowers, coconuts, fruit salad, and pink cake.

Emily

Emily is a blue haired woman who lives with her sister Haley and works at the Stardrop Saloon alongside Gus. She loves the spiritual and witchy things in life, and while she can be a bit airheaded, she's very sweet. Her birthday is Spring 27, and she visits Harvey's Clinic Winter 11. Her loved items are Amethyst, Ruby, Aquamarine, Cloth, Emerald, Jade, Survival Burger, Topaz, and Wool.

Leah

Leah is a creative, hardworking artist who loves spending her time outside foraging and sketching. She lives in a small cottage in Cindersap Forest by herself. Her birthday is Winter 23, and she visits Harvey's Clinic Spring 16. Her loved gifts are goat cheese, wine, salad, poppyseed muffin, stir fry, truffle, and vegetable medley.

Penny

Penny is a sweet, shy, caring young woman who loves to read. She lives in a trailer in Pelican Town with her mother Pam, and teaches Jas and Vincent at the library. Her birthday is Fall 2, and she visits Harvey's Clinic Winter 4. Her loved gifts are diamonds, melons, emeralds, poppies, poppyseed muffins, red plate, roots plate, sandfish, and tom kha soup.

Alex

Alex is a boastful, sporty young man who spends his time tossing his gridball ball and flirting with Haley. He lives near the Saloon with Evelyn and George, his grandparents. His birthday is Summer 13 and his clinic visit is Summer 16. Alex's loved gifts are Complete Breakfast. Salmon Dinner, and Pearl.

Elliott

Elliott is a mature, handsome young writer who spends much of his time doing just that. He lives in the cabin at the beach by himself. His birthday is Fall 5, and his clinic visit is Summer 9. His loved gift items are crab cakes, duck feathers, squid ink, pomegranate, Tom Kha soup, and lobster.

Harvey

Harvey is a responsible, hard working man who is employed as the town's doctor. He's one of the oldest of the bachelors. Harvey lives above the clinic where on his own time he contacts planes flying by. His birthday is Winter 14 and his loved gifts are pickles, coffee, wine, Super meal, and truffle oil.

Sam

Sam is an outgoing guy who plays the guitar and works at Joja Mart. He lives with his mother Jodi, brother Vincent, and father Kent (Kent only year 2 and on) next to Haley and Emily's house. His birthday is Summer 17, and he visits Harvey's clinic Fall 11. His loved gifts are pizza, cactus fruit, maple bar, and tigerseye.

Sebastian

Sebastian is a quiet, angsty computer nerd who stays in his family's basement, only usually leaving to hang out with his friends, or to stand by the lake and smoke. His birthday is Winter 11, and he visits the clinic Summer 4. His loved gifts are Obsidian, void egg, sashimi, frozen tear, and pumpkin soup.

Shane

Shane is at first a rude and unhappy man who works at Joja Mart and drinks his emotions away. If you get closer to him though, you may find that there's something sweet behind that tough exterior. Cluck cluck. Shane's birthday is Spring 20 and he doesn't have a yearly clinic visit. He lives at his Aunt Marnies ranch and takes care of his god daughter, Jas, when he's not at work or at the tavern. His loved gifts are beer, pizza, hot peppers, and pepper poppers.

Non Marriageable Characters

Caroline

Caroline is a sweet woman who lives in Pierre's general store with her husband Pierre (icky) and Abigail, her daughter. Her birthday is Winter 7,she visits the clinic Fall 25. Her loved gifts are fish tacos, summer spangles, green tea, and tropical curry.

Clint

Clint is an interesting, odd older man who is the town blacksmith. He's got a crush on Haley's sister Emily, and he's a little too obsessed. His birthday is Winter 26 and his clinic visit is Winter 16. His loved gifts are fiddlehead rissotto, jade, amethyst, emerald, aquamarine, artichoke dip, gold bar, iridium bar, omni geode, ruby, and topaz.

Demetrius

Demetrius is an intelligent scientist who lives in the cabin on the mountain with his wife Robin, daughter Maru, and step-son Sebastian. His birthday is Summer 19, and he visits the clinic Summer 25. His loved gifts are Ice Cream, Strawberry, Bean Hotpot, and Rice pudding.

Evelyn

Evelyn is a kind little old lady who lives in a house next to the Saloon. She lives there with her grandson Alex and Husband George. Winter 20 is her birthday, and she visits the clinic every season on the 2nd by herself and the 23rd with George. Her favorite items are beets,chocolate cake, diamonds, fairy rose, raisins, stuffing, anf tulips.

George

George is a grumpy old fart who lives with his wife Evelyn and his grandson Alex in a house by the Saloon. His birthday is Fall 24 and he visits the clinic with Evelyn every season on the 23rd. His loved gifts are leeks and fried mushroom.

Gus

Gus is the friendly, hardworking, owner of the Stardrop Saloon. He employs Emily part time to help him and provides lots of helpful recipes. His birthday is Summer 8 and his clinic visit is Fall 4. His loved gift items are tropical curry, escargot, fish tacos, diamonds, and oranges.

Jas

Jas is Marnie's daughter, and Shane's Goddaughter. She's often seen skipping rope at the big tree in Cindersap forest and hanging out with Vincent as Penny teaches the two. Her birthday is Summer 4, and she visits Harvey's Clinic Winter 18.

Jodi

Jodi is the mother of Sam and Vincent, and the wife of Kent. They all live togehter in the bottom left area of Pelican Town. Her birthday is Fall 11, and she visits the clinic on Spring 18 for herself, and Spring 11 for Vincent. Her favorite items are Chocolate cake, crispy bass, diamonds, eggplant parmesan, fried eel, pancakes, rhubarb pie, and vegetable medley.

Kent

Kent is the father of Sam and Vincent and the doting husband of Jodi. His birthday is Spring 4, and he loves Fiddlehead Risotto and roasted hazelnuts.

Lewis

Lewis is the town mayor, he lives in the Mayor's Manor next to the Stardrop Saloon. He is in a secret relationship with Marnie. His birthday is Spring 7, and he visits the clinic Fall 9. He loves green tea, autumn's bounty, glazed yams, hot peppers, and vegetable medley.
